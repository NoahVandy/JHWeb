Jacob Harding

Jacob has been a dedicated filmmaker since he picked up his first camera when he was just twelve years old, and since then has worked on over 200 projects! While he’s always loved making films, directing really became his passion once he got into highschool and started to take filmmaking very seriously. Out of this work effort he went on to direct his first feature film, XYZ (2018), which had most of its overall production done while he was just fresh out of Highschool. This passion grew into the disturbing yet hilarious short film Meet The McCrory’s (2018) and into his latest film, Withdrawal (2020), the story of an addict going through recovery and the hardships it brings on himself and those closest to him. Every film directed by Jacob he also has had the pleasure to produce and write as well, giving him a look into the entirety of the film. Jacob soon grew out into more of a producer role, as he took over as Executive Producer/Showrunner of After Curfew (2016-) and has held that position for its third and upcoming fourth seasons, bringing on a total of 100 members into the show’s ranks. Through this producing role, Jacob was able to produce short films such as Wayfarer, In My Mind, The Walker, Distressed, and Life Goes On. Jacob is passionate as is he determined in every film he gets to work on. Filmmaking is everything to him, and he strives to make sure that everyone within his films has their own creative freedoms and that it’s a fluid creative environment where everyone can be involved, instead of just one person calling the shots.
